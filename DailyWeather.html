AREA DISCUSSION

SYNOPSIS
Temperatures will continue well above average to start the new week. A weather system brushing by to our north will bring some cooling Tuesday with another system bringing additional cooling Friday. Breezy at times with these systems, but precip chances are low for the southeast corner of the state..

DISCUSSION
One more day with near record afternoon highs as a shortwave ridge phases with the northern remains of the strong high pressure now anchored in Mexico. A trough will push through the region Monday with most of the cooling in SE AZ trailing the rest of the state into Tuesday. Breezy conditions but limited moisture this far south. A shortwave ridge Wednesday will push temperatures up a few degrees, but a deeper split in the flow later in the week will not give much time to warm up again. Ensembles argue for a significant weakness over the desert southwest late next week. Much cooler temperatures a sure bet, falling below seasonal averages for a brief period Friday through next weekend. Gusty winds at times, but a split with a dry inland trajectory like this one looks to be usually doesn't have time to tap into much moisture. As long as it doesn't get cut off and left behind, precip chances will again be minimal this far south.
